About Me

Why did I make this site? How to get to Oxford Falls Waterfall Sadly, there is no public transport access to the waterfall, which makes it hard to access if a motor vehicle is unavailable. The closest bus stop to the Waterfall is Forest Way Shopping Centre, Frenchs Forest.
